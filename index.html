How to Use This Tool

  1. Paste your main timeline data into the first text area below.
  2. Paste your additional timeline data (Ordered at, Send to restaurant) into the second text area.
  3. Select the rider status, enter the delivery time, and select the order type below the text areas.
  4. Click Generate Timeline to view both timelines, total dispatching time, and total waiting time at the restaurant, etc.

This tool detects delivery milestones, calculates dispatching time, and determines delay reasons based on dispatching, rider availability, partner preparation, or reassignment to another rider.
